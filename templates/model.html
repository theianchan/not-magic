About This Dataset

This dataset shows sales of a particular product in 200 different markets, along with advertising budgets for the product in each of those markets for three different media: TV, radio, and newspaper.

This dataset (as far as I can tell) is from Introduction to Statistical Learning by Gareth James, Daniela Witten, Trevor Hastie, and Robert Tibshirani. I see it all over the internet but no one seems to have the actual source.
